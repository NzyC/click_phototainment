Top 10 Photobooth Companies in Sydney (2025 Edition)

If you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or private party and want to add something fun and unforgettable — hiring a photobooth is a no-brainer. With so many providers in the city, finding the right Sydney photobooth or 360 booth can be tough, especially when you're searching for quality, value, and reliability.

To help, we’ve compiled a list of the 10 best photobooth companies in Sydney, including a mix of classic booths, modern 360 booths, and services that cover everywhere from the CBD to Western Sydney.

🥇 1. Clickk Phototainment

When it comes to value, service, and experience — Clickk Phototainment is the best in the business. Known for their modern, sleek booth designs and flawless execution, Clickk offers both classic photobooth hire and 360 booth hire across Sydney.

What makes Clickk stand out is their commitment to customer satisfaction. Clients consistently praise the smooth communication, quick responses, and friendly on-site staff. Every booth comes with professionally designed templates, unlimited sessions, and fun props — and their 360 booths capture stunning slow-motion videos that are instantly shareable on social media.

Clickk is also one of the few companies that provide Western Sydney photobooth coverage with no compromise on quality. Whether you're hosting a wedding in the Blue Mountains or a brand activation in the CBD, Clickk delivers top-tier service at the best value for money in Sydney.

2. Snappy Photobooths

A well-established provider, Snappy Photobooths offers a range of photobooth options including enclosed, open-air, and mirror booths. They’re known for their sleek setups and professional presentation. Great choice for weddings and large corporate events in the city.

3. The Party Starters

Operating mainly in the Inner West and Eastern Suburbs, The Party Starters offer both classic and GIF-style photobooths with a huge selection of backdrops and props. They’ve gained popularity for their party packages and flexible setups.

4. Photo Corner

Photo Corner is a boutique-style provider that specializes in elegant photobooth experiences, perfect for weddings and upmarket events. Their minimalist setups are great for venues with limited space but a premium look.

5. Social Exposure

For those after something highly branded or marketing-driven, Social Exposure focuses on content creation through 360 booths, roaming photography, and digital GIF booths. Perfect for fashion launches, influencer events, and branded pop-ups.

6. SnixPix

SnixPix offers a fun, retro-style photobooth service with quirky props and customised print templates. Based in Western Sydney, they’re ideal for birthdays, community events, and casual gatherings with a laid-back vibe.

7. The Selfie Station Co.

The Selfie Station Co. offers portable selfie pod booths that are perfect for events with limited space or roaming activations. Their digital booths are especially popular for school formals and modern brand events.

8. OMG Booths

Based in the Hills District, OMG Booths offers 360 video booths and enclosed photobooths with high-end lighting and video effects. Their team focuses on larger-scale weddings and formal events.

9. Vivid Booths

Vivid Booths is known for their 360 booth hire in Sydney, with a strong portfolio of corporate clients. Their setups often include LED rings, branded overlays, and instant social media sharing stations.

10. Mirror Me Magic

Mirror Me Magic offers interactive mirror booths that let guests sign photos, play animations, and create dynamic photo experiences. They’re a good fit for elegant weddings or kids’ parties with tech-savvy guests.

Why Photobooths Are a Must-Have at Sydney Events

Adding a photobooth — whether it's a traditional photo booth or a modern Sydney 360 booth — transforms your event from good to unforgettable. Guests love taking home printed photo strips, and social sharing from 360 booths can give your brand or wedding real visibility.
