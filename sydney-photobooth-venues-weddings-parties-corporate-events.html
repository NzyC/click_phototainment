Top Sydney Venues for Photobooth & 360 Booth Hire

Looking for the perfect venue and entertainment combo? Whether you're in the Sydney CBD, Eastern Suburbs, Western Sydney, or Fairfield, here are 40 top venues where our photobooths and 360 booths have brought the fun:

Sydney CBD & Inner City

  • The Grounds of the City – Heritage grandeur meets photobooth creativity—perfect for vintage themes and classy weddings.
  • Hyatt Regency (Darling Harbour) – Elegant receptions shine with our 360 booth and tailored print options.
  • The Establishment Ballroom – Sleek interiors match our minimalist booth setups and monochrome backdrops.
  • Luna Park Venues (Milsons Point) – Funfair energy captured beautifully in bright props and vibrant prints.
  • Ivy Ballroom (George Street) – Glamour matches our sequin backdrops and gold-themed props.
  • Museum of Contemporary Art (Circular Quay) – Modern art surroundings complement our sleek and artistic booth styles.
  • Quay Restaurant (Opera House precinct) – Scenic elegance for weddings, enhanced by monogrammed templates.
  • Sofitel Sydney Wentworth – Black-tie elegance enhanced with luxe booth styling.

Eastern Suburbs & Coastal Areas

  • Bondi Icebergs – Beachside charm at sunset, perfect for candid sunset photo sessions.
  • Coogee Bay Hotel – Relaxed coastal vibes enhanced by boho themes and laid-back setups.
  • Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel – Seaside elegance meets floral-themed printed memories.
  • Clovelly Surf Club – Compact venue ideal for intimate photo fun and group shots.

  • Doltone House (Hyde Park & Woolloomooloo) – Formal corporate events and weddings thrive with photo and 360 booth setups.

Western Sydney & Greater West

  • The Highline Venue (Bankstown) – Luxurious interiors and high-ceiling spaces highlight our 360 booth’s cinematic style.
  • Waterview (Bicentennial Park, Homebush) – Natural surrounds work beautifully with floral archways and backdrop framing.
  • The William Inglis Hotel (Warwick Farm) – Versatile venue for weddings, receptions, and onsite 360 booth fun.
  • Fairmont St Events (Lakemba) – Cultural celebrations benefit from enclosed booths and themed prints.
  • Lantana Venues (Bonnyrigg) – Budget-friendly meets photo entertainment—fun for birthdays and baptisms.
  • The Renaissance (Lidcombe) – Refined receptions with generous booth space and lighting setups.
  • Clarence House (Belmore) – Elegant packages with guestbook options for mementos.
  • Bankstown Sports Club – All-in-one events, birthday parties, and social functions suit our open-air setups.
  • Novella on the Park (Fairfield) – A local favourite, where urban charm meets casual celebrations—perfect for themed booths.
  • Fairfield RSL Club (Fairfield) – Popular for milestone parties—our booths add instant entertainment and memories.

Parramatta & Inner West

North Sydney & Northern Beaches
