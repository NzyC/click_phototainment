🎥 What Is a 360 Booth?

Unlike a traditional photobooth, a 360 video booth captures a full slow-motion video of your guests standing on a platform while a camera rotates around them. It creates a stunning, cinematic experience that’s fun in the moment and even better to share online.

The end result? A high-quality, slow-motion video that guests can instantly download and post to Instagram, TikTok, or WhatsApp — making it the perfect feature for both private and promotional events.
