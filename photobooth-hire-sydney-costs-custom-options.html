Photobooth Hire in Sydney: What’s Included, Costs & Customisation Options

If you're planning an event in Sydney and looking to add something fun, interactive, and unforgettable — hiring a photobooth is a brilliant choice. At Clickk, we provide premium photobooth hire across Sydney with flexible packages designed for weddings, corporate events, school formals, birthdays, and more.

In this guide, we’ll walk you through exactly what’s included in our photobooth hire, how much it costs, and all the ways you can customise your experience with Clickk.

What’s Included in Clickk’s Photobooth Hire

Every Clickk photobooth package is designed to deliver quality, convenience, and entertainment. Here’s what’s typically included:

  • Unlimited Photo Sessions – Guests can snap as many photos as they like during the hire period.
  • Instant Photo Prints – Everyone gets to take home a high-quality, branded print strip.
  • Customisable Print Templates – Include your names, logo, event hashtag, or custom colours.
  • Props & Accessories – Hats, glasses, signs, boas – fun items that bring every photo to life.
  • Professional Setup & Pack Down – We deliver, set up, and remove everything – no stress for you.
  • Friendly Booth Attendant – A Clickk team member stays on-site to assist guests and manage the flow.
  • Online Gallery – Get access to all digital files after the event via a private link.

Photobooth Hire Costs in Sydney

The cost of hiring a photobooth in Sydney typically depends on a few key factors:

  • Duration of hire (e.g., 2 hours vs. 5 hours)
  • Type of booth (Classic vs. 360 Infinity Booth)
  • Event date and location
  • Add-ons (e.g., guestbook, premium backdrop, roaming photographer)

At Clickk, our photobooth packages start from $699 for smaller events and can scale up depending on your needs. We also offer custom quotes for large-scale or corporate activations.
